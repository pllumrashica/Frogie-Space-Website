Frogie Space is a Pristina-based studio that combines the rootedness of a local architecture firm with the broad interests of an international design studio. We strive to achieve surprisingly simple and meaningful built design and developing environments that are unforgettable yet performative, remaining always, site-specific, responsive to the current conditions and cost effective.
